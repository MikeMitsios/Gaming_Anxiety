General information about the dashboard
Charts

Our Dashboard consists of five different charts and each one displays a different relation between the questionairee scores and the chosen variables (columns of our dataset).
  • The first chart (Number of Individuals per Score) is a line-chart which represents the distribution of the population according to the different questionairee scores(GAD,SPIN,SWL).
  • The second chart (Average SWL Score per Hours and work) is a multi-category line-chart which shows the relation of the SWL questionairee-score with both work condition and gaming-hours per week.
  • The third chart (Scores per Rason of Playing) is a multi-category bar-chart that displays all three questionairee scores in accordance with the reason of playing games.
  • The forth chart (Average SPIN per Playstyle) is a bubble-chart which represents the average SPIN questionairee-score per playstyle.
  • The fifth and final chart (Distribution of Data across the World) is a map that represents the distribution of the data across the world.

Filters

The dashboards contains a wide variety of filters, so that any reader can come to their own conclusions.
